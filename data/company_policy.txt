ABC Technologies Employee Handbook

Working Hours
Employees are expected to work from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM from Monday to Friday.

Leave Policy
Each employee is entitled to 20 paid leave days every calendar year.

Medical Leave
Employees can avail up to 10 days of medical leave with valid medical certificates.

Remote Work
Employees are allowed to work remotely for up to three days per week with manager approval.

Dress Code
Business casual attire is required from Monday to Thursday. Fridays are casual dress days.

Internet Usage
Company internet resources should only be used for work-related activities.

Salary
Salary is credited on the last working day of every month.

Performance Review
Employee performance reviews are conducted every six months.

Notice Period
The standard notice period is 60 days.

Office Location
The headquarters is located in Bangalore, India.
